Job Description

Tech Solutions Ethiopia is looking for an experienced Senior Software Engineer to join our growing team. You will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining high-quality software solutions that meet our clients' needs and drive innovation in Ethiopia's tech ecosystem.

As a key member of our engineering team, you'll work on challenging projects that impact thousands of users across Africa. We're looking for someone passionate about building scalable solutions that solve real-world problems in our local context.

About Our Company

Tech Solutions Ethiopia is a leading software development company based in Addis Ababa, with offices in Nairobi and Dubai. Founded in 2015, we've grown from a small startup to a team of 150+ professionals serving clients across Africa and the Middle East.

Our mission is to empower African businesses through innovative technology solutions. We specialize in enterprise software, mobile applications, and cloud solutions tailored for the African market.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement scalable and maintainable software solutions using modern technologies
  • Lead the development of new features from concept to deployment
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including product managers, designers, and QA engineers
  • Write clean, efficient, and well-documented code following best practices
  • Mentor junior developers and conduct thorough code reviews
  • Identify and correct performance bottlenecks and fix bugs
  • Stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and industry trends relevant to the African tech ecosystem
  • Participate in architectural decisions and technical roadmap planning

Requirements & Qualifications

Education

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or related field (Master's preferred)

Experience

5+ years of professional software development experience with at least 2 years in a senior role

Technical Skills

Expertise in JavaScript/TypeScript, Node.js, React, and modern web technologies. Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP) required.

Soft Skills

Excellent problem-solving, communication, and teamwork skills. Ability to mentor junior developers and collaborate effectively.
